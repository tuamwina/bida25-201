About Us

Letsha Sanctuary Lodge began as a cultural retreat and has since evolved into a modern lodge offering accommodation, leisure activities, and event hosting services.

Located approximately 25-30 km east of Maun, Botswana, the lodge provides a unique setting for both leisure and business travelers. It is ideally positioned for exploring the Okavango Delta, which is known the world's most renowned inland ecosystems, as well as the nearby Moremi Game Reserve for its rich wildlife

The remote location is situated away from all man-made distractions allowing one to truly immerse in the pristine natural surroundings and enjoy unrivalled wildlife encounters. Whether you're planning to visit one of Botswana's incredible game reserves or a corporate retreat, Letsha Sanctuary Lodge will host your group with our signature warmth and hospitality.
