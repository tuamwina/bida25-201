We strive to maintain an atmosphere of profound peace and respect for the natural environment, We ask all guests to read and adhere to these guidelines during their stay.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation made in 10 daysbefore arrival, clients would receive a a full refund.

Cancellation made within 5 days before arrival, clients would receive a 50% refund.

Cancellation made within 3 days before arrival, there would not be any refundable.

Check-in and check-out

Check-in time is from 3:00 PM till. Early check-in is available upon request.

Check-out time is at 11:00 AM, late checkout may be charged at an additional price.

All guests must present valid identification upon arrival.

Noise and Quiet Hours

Quite hours are from 10:00PM to 7:00AMin all indoor areas.

Please be mindful of other guest when moving between cabin areas at night.

No mucic is permitted outside the time frame stated.

Smoking and Alcohol

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside all cabins or any areas with a "No Smoking Sign.

Designated smoking areas are available in the eastern side of the lodge, near the car parking area.

Alcohol is permitted in all guest areas, Please drink responsibily.

Pets

Well behaved dogs are welcome in designated pet-friendly areas.

Anon-refundable pet fee of BWP 150.00 per stay.

Pets are not permitted allowed in the dining hall and at the Library.

Pets must be kept on a leash in all communial areas.

Respect for Nature

Do not feed or approach any wildlife on the property or trails.

Picking wildflowers or disturbing plants is not permitted.

Please take all rubbish back to the lodge No littering on trails.

Campfires are only permitted in the designated firepit area.
